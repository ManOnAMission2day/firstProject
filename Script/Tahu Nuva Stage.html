Tahu Nuva Script

Tahu Nuva's Stage
  • Chronicler: The mask I found was the Mask of Light. The Turaga sent me on a quest to find the Seventh Toa.
  • Tahu Nuva: Seventh Toa?
  • Chronicler: Only the Seventh Toa can wear the mask.
  • Tahu Nuva: And where is the Seventh Toa?
  • Chronicler: I dont know and now i've lost the nas to those creatures and they're heading to destroy the Koro!
  • Pohatu Nuva: The Turaga say they are Rahkshi - the sons of the Makuta. Deadliest of foes.
  • Tahu Nuva: Makuta! Brother. See the Chronicler to safety. I will deal with the Rahkshi.

    • Tahu Nuva then has a lava race to the Koro, in which Tahu wins.

    End of Tahu Nuva's Stage.
